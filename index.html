About Us

One of our longest-running collaborations is Smarta, a borehole monitoring platform we co-developed with a South African water company. From the early days, Mistbits designed and built the custom hardware, firmware, backend, web dashboard, and mobile app — while both our companies grew alongside each other.

Now, we're building the Mistbits Whisper Network — our own nationwide, Meshtastic-powered infrastructure designed for low-power IoT deployments across South Africa. We'll be launching a range of in-house devices to run on it soon.
