About Mistbits

Software that stays useful.

We are a small South African software company that builds connected platforms for real operations, then keeps them running and improving.

Where we work

Close to the people using the system.

Mistbits grew out of a practical need: make remote water infrastructure easier to see, understand and operate.

Our longest-running work is around water. We have built software for borehole and reservoir operations, remote meter readings, leak visibility, reporting, analytics and billing. That experience has taught us how much the quality of an IoT platform depends on understanding what happens beyond the screen.

We work directly with the people responsible for the system. Their feedback shapes the apps, automation and reports, while our long-term involvement keeps the software dependable as the operation changes.

How we think

Keep it clear. Keep it working.

Connected systems are complicated enough. The software around them should make the operation simpler.

01

Useful over impressive

Every screen, alert and report should help someone understand the system or decide what to do next.

02

Reliability over novelty

We favour understandable behaviour, good monitoring and easy recovery over unnecessary complexity.

03

Partnership over handover

We stay close after launch because the best improvements come from seeing the platform used in practice.
