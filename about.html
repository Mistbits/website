About Us

One of our longest-running collaborations is Smarta, a borehole monitoring platform we co-developed with a South African water company. From the early days, Mistbits designed and built the custom hardware, firmware, backend, web dashboard, and mobile app — while both our companies grew alongside each other.

Now, we're building the Mistbits Whisper Network — our own nationwide, Meshtastic-powered infrastructure designed for low-power IoT deployments across South Africa. We'll be launching a range of in-house devices to run on it soon.

What We Do

We design and build connected systems — including devices, embedded code, data pipelines, and dashboards. Our work spans from field hardware to cloud interfaces, built mostly from scratch, and mostly in-house.

Why We Started

Mistbits grew out of a need to solve practical problems with reliable, low-power tech. We care more about uptime than headlines, and we prefer tools that are quiet, resilient, and purpose-built.

Looking Ahead

The Whisper Network is a foundation — not a product. We're experimenting with new kinds of edge-connected devices, networked tools, and open protocols to support off-grid IoT in remote places.

Work With Us

We’re open to working with people building useful, honest systems — whether it's a field deployment, device integration, or something experimental.
